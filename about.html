About Us

Empowering Healthier Lifestyles

BMICalc is dedicated to providing premium, scientific, and highly accurate calculators to help you track your body metrics and daily nutritional goals with absolute clarity.

Our Mission

At BMICalc, we believe that health metrics should be easily accessible, simple to understand, and completely free. We design interactive, premium utility tools based on established medical formulas to help you analyze your Body Mass Index (BMI), Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), and Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) with confidence.

Scientific Integrity First

Our application isn't built on placeholders or generic estimates. We implement standard, verified medical formulas used by the World Health Organization (WHO), Center for Disease Control (CDC), and registered clinical dietitians globally:

  • Body Mass Index (BMI): Formulated via kg/m² (or the imperial scaled equivalent) to check body weight category boundaries accurately.
  • Ideal Body Weight (IBW): Derived using the Devine Formula, factoring in biological gender tallies for bone and skeletal structures.
  • Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR): Calculated using the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation, recognized by nutritional sciences as the gold standard for metabolic rate estimation.
  • Active Calories (TDEE): Factored using active multipliers spanning five precise physical exertion categories.
