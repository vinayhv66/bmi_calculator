Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Our Priority

BMICalc values your data privacy. Learn why our web calculator is completely secure, private, and runs entirely in your web browser.

1. Zero Server-Side Storage

Unlike standard health trackers, BMICalc does not send your personal measurements (like age, gender, height, or weight) to external database servers. All computations are conducted locally in your web browser utilizing client-side JavaScript. This ensures that your information never leaves your personal device.

2. Use of LocalStorage

To enable cross-page calculations (such as auto-filling the daily calories form with your BMI parameters), we use the browser's native localStorage API. This data remains on your physical machine and is accessible only via your browser. You can clear this data at any point simply by purging your browser cookies or database cache.

3. Third-Party Analytics

We may collect anonymized traffic metrics (like pages visited or screen sizes) to improve design responsiveness. No personally identifiable details are collected. We do not run tracker cookies or sell data to advertisers.

4. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy statement, you can reach out via the Contact Us form, and we will get back to you within 7 days.
